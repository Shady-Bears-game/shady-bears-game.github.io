Winning Tips for Shady Bears

Map Awareness

Shadows spread in patterns—watch for fronts that close corridors. Keep an escape lane open and avoid dead ends.

Route Planning

Work in loops that pass multiple acorn clusters to keep momentum. If the shade cuts you off, pivot to a shorter loop quickly.

Power‑Up Timing

Don’t grab a speed boost when you’re already safe—save it for when a shadow traps you or to snag a big acorn pile.

Co‑op Coordination

  • Assign zones so players don’t collide or waste routes.
  • Use pings or short calls to warn about closing paths.
  • One player can “pull” the shade while the other clears a cluster.
